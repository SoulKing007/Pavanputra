What to do in Dwarka

Visit Dwarkadhish Temple for blessings, then go to Bet Dwarka.

Relax at Shivrajpur Beach and visit Rukmini Devi Temple.

Walk across Sudama Setu for scenic views.

Witness Aarti at Dwarkadhish Temple and Gomti Ghat for a holy dip.

Explore Nageshwar Jyotirlinga, one of Shiva’s 12 sacred shrines.

Visit Gopi Talav, a serene lake linked to Krishna’s pastimes.

Discover Bhadkeshwar Mahadev Temple, surrounded by the sea.

Enjoy local Gujarati cuisine and shop for souvenirs.

About Dwarka

Dwarka, one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites, is an ancient city in Gujarat, believed to be Lord Krishna’s kingdom. It is home to the Dwarkadhish Temple, Bet Dwarka, and the sacred Gomti Ghat. The city is rich in mythology, temples, and coastal beauty, attracting devotees and tourists alike.
