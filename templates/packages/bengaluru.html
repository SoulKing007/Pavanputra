About Bengaluru

Bengaluru, the capital of Karnataka, is known as the "Silicon Valley of India" due to its status as a leading tech hub. The city is a perfect blend of modern innovation and cultural heritage, boasting a vibrant nightlife, lush parks, and historic landmarks.
