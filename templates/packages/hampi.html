About Hampi

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Karnataka, is renowned for its stunning ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire. Once a thriving medieval capital, it now showcases majestic temples, intricate carvings, and grand structures. Surrounded by boulder-strewn landscapes and the Tungabhadra River, Hampi is a paradise for history lovers and explorers.
