What to do in Leh Ladakh

Leh Ladakh offers a blend of natural beauty and cultural experiences.

Visit the serene Pangong Tso Lake, known for its changing hues.

Explore the historic Leh Palace, offering panoramic city views.

Experience tranquility at Thiksey Monastery, a prominent Buddhist site.

Embark on the Markha Valley trek for adventure enthusiasts.

Witness the confluence of the Indus and Zanskar rivers.

Savor traditional Ladakhi cuisine and engage with the warm local community.

About Leh Ladakh

Leh-Ladakh is a breathtaking region in northern India, known for its stunning landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and adventurous roads. It is part of the Union Territory of Ladakh, which was formerly a part of Jammu and Kashmir before becoming a separate entity in 2019.
