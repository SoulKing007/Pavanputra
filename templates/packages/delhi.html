What to do in Delhi

Witness the grandeur of the Republic Day parade at Rajpath.

Explore the historic Red Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Visit India Gate, a tribute to soldiers who sacrificed their lives.

Experience spirituality at the Akshardham Temple and Lotus Temple.

Stroll through Chandni Chowk and savor Delhi’s famous street food.

Discover the Mughal architecture of Humayun’s Tomb and Qutub Minar.

Enjoy a shopping spree at Connaught Place and Dilli Haat.

Take a rickshaw ride in Old Delhi for a glimpse of its vibrant culture.

Visit the Rashtrapati Bhavan and Parliament House.

Relax at Lodhi Garden or Hauz Khas Village.

About Delhi

Delhi, the capital of India, is a vibrant metropolis that seamlessly blends history with modernity. Rich in cultural heritage, it is home to iconic landmarks such as the Red Fort, India Gate, Qutub Minar, and Humayun’s Tomb.
