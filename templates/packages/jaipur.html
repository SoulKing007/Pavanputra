What to Do in Jaipur

Explore the magnificent historical landmarks like Amber Fort and City Palace.

Visit the famous Hawa Mahal and admire its stunning architecture.

Enjoy adventure activities like a thrilling hot air balloon ride over the Pink City.

Trek through the scenic trails around Nahargarh Fort for a panoramic city view.

Experience the serenity of beautiful lakes like Man Sagar Lake near Jal Mahal.

Seek blessings at ancient temples like Govind Dev Ji Temple and Birla Mandir.

Witness the breathtaking beauty of Jaipur’s royal heritage at Jaigarh and Nahargarh Forts.

About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is famously known as the Pink City due to its uniquely colored buildings. It is home to stunning historical landmarks like Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, and City Palace, which showcase the city’s rich heritage and architectural brilliance.
