What to Do in Chennai

Explore the scenic Marina Beach, one of the longest urban beaches in the world.

Visit the historic Kapaleeshwarar Temple, known for its Dravidian architecture.

Admire the colonial-era charm of Fort St. George and its museum.

Experience spiritual serenity at San Thome Basilica, a famous pilgrimage site.

Discover Chennai’s artistic heritage at Kalakshetra Foundation.

Shop for exquisite silk sarees at T. Nagar’s bustling markets.

Enjoy authentic South Indian delicacies at popular eateries like Saravana Bhavan.

Explore the Government Museum, home to rare artifacts and sculptures.

Watch a Bharatanatyam performance, a classical dance form of Tamil Nadu.

Take a relaxing stroll along Elliot’s Beach in Besant Nagar.

About Chennai

Chennai, the capital of Tamil Nadu, is a fascinating tourist destination that blends history, culture, and modern attractions. Known for its stunning coastline, Marina Beach is one of the longest urban beaches in the world, offering breathtaking sunrise views. The city is also home to magnificent temples like the Kapaleeshwarar Temple and historic churches such as San Thome Basilica.
