About Kerala

Kerala, located on India's southwestern Malabar Coast, is renowned for its lush greenery, tranquil backwaters, and rich cultural heritage. Often referred to as "God's Own Country," the state boasts a high literacy rate and diverse traditions. Tourists are drawn to its serene landscapes, vibrant festivals, and classical art forms like Kathakali. Kerala's unique blend of natural beauty and cultural richness makes it a captivating destination.
