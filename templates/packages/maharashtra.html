About Maharashtra

Maharashtra, a state in western India, is known for its diverse culture, rich history, and stunning natural beauty. From the bustling streets of Mumbai to the serene beaches and hill stations like Lonavala and Matheran, Maharashtra offers a wide range of experiences for travelers.
