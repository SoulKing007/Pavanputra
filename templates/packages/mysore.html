What to do in Mysore

Visit the majestic Mysore Palace, a symbol of royal heritage.

Explore Chamundi Hill and seek blessings at Chamundeshwari Temple.

Stroll through Brindavan Gardens and enjoy the musical fountain show.

Discover Mysore Zoo, home to diverse wildlife and exotic species.

Admire art and history at Jaganmohan Palace and its art gallery.

Shop for Mysore silk sarees, sandalwood, and handcrafted souvenirs.

Savor the famous Mysore Pak and other traditional delicacies.

About Mysore

Mysore, known as the cultural capital of Karnataka, is famous for its grand Mysore Palace, rich heritage, and vibrant Dasara festival. The city is renowned for its royal history, silk sarees, sandalwood, and traditional arts. Visitors can explore Chamundi Hill, scenic gardens, and historic temples, making it a perfect blend of tradition and modernity.
